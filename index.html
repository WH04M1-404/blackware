Welcome to Blackware Cybersecurity

About Us

BL4CKW4R3 is a cutting-edge cybersecurity company specializing in offensive security and red team engagements. Our primary mission is to simulate real-world cyberattacks on organizations to uncover vulnerabilities before malicious actors exploit them.

We deliver comprehensive red team operations, ranging from phishing campaigns and social engineering attacks to full-scale network compromise exercises. Our experts work hand-in-hand with clients to ensure that their digital assets remain secure against advanced threats.

Beyond red team engagements, we also provide offensive security training designed to transform beginners into professional penetration testers and red team operators through a progressive and hands-on learning experience.

Penetration Testing Services

At BL4CKW4R3, we go beyond traditional penetration testing. Our red team engagements cover:

  • Web Applications: Identifying critical vulnerabilities in websites, APIs, and online services.
  • Corporate Infrastructure: Assessing internal networks, cloud platforms, and hybrid systems.

What sets us apart is our "no-find, no-fee" model: We do not charge for initial scans. Payment is only required if we successfully identify exploitable vulnerabilities. This ensures we are fully committed to delivering tangible results and helps build trust with clients who want to test their security posture without financial risk.

Our Certifications

JRRT - JUNIOR RED TEAMER

The Junior Red Teamer (JRRT) certification validates foundational knowledge and practical skills in offensive security and ethical hacking. It is designed for beginners who want to enter the world of penetration testing and red teaming, offering a clear starting point into the tools, techniques, and concepts used by security professionals. Through this certification, candidates will learn to identify vulnerabilities, perform basic security assessments, and understand key principles of ethical hacking across both network and web application environments. Key Areas Covered: Kali Linux & Security Tools: Familiarity with Kali Linux and essential tools like Nmap, Metasploit, and BeEF-XSS for scanning, exploitation, and client-side attacks. Web Application Attacks: Understanding common vulnerabilities such as SQL Injection (SQLi), Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), and other OWASP Top 10 threats. Networking Basics: Core networking concepts, protocols, and traffic analysis for security testing.

RTP - RED TEAM PROFFESINAL

The Red Team Professional (RTP) certification is designed for individuals seeking to master advanced offensive security techniques and real-world red teaming operations. Building upon foundational skills, this certification validates a candidate’s ability to execute complex, stealthy attacks against modern enterprise environments while simulating real adversary tactics. Through this certification, participants gain the expertise to bypass security defenses, escalate privileges across diverse platforms, and compromise high-value systems, including those in hardened or isolated environments. Key Areas Covered: Evasion Techniques: Defeating firewalls, IDS/IPS, EDR, and antivirus systems to maintain stealth. Privilege Escalation: Advanced attacks on Windows, Linux, and Android systems to achieve persistence and control. Hardware & Wireless Exploits: Drone hijacking, Bluetooth-based attacks, and methods to breach air-gapped networks. Social Engineering at Scale: Mass phishing campaigns to compromise users and gain initial access. The RTP certification represents a higher-level credential for professionals aiming to lead red team engagements and advanced security assessments.

RTE - RED TEAM EXPERT

The Red Teaming Expert (RTE) certification represents the highest level of achievement in offensive security training at BL4CKW4R3. Designed for seasoned professionals, this certification validates the skills needed to conduct sophisticated, full-scope red team operations against highly secured and complex environments. Candidates pursuing the RTE credential demonstrate their ability to simulate advanced persistent threats (APTs), exploit cutting-edge technologies, and execute end-to-end attack chains with precision and stealth. Key Areas Covered: Automotive Exploitation: Hacking cars, Tesla vehicles, and connected transportation systems. Telecommunication Attacks: Exploiting base transceiver stations (BTS) and deploying fake BTS for interception and control. IoT & Smart Home Intrusions: Compromising smart house devices and automation systems. Malware Development: Creating and deploying information stealers and custom ransomware payloads. Enterprise Exploitation: Advanced Active Directory attacks for lateral movement and full domain compromise. The RTE certification demonstrates expert-level offensive capabilities, equipping professionals with the knowledge to challenge modern security infrastructures at the highest standard.
